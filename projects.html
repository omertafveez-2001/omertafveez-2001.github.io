Sparse-Autoencoders Guided FitNets

Muhammad Khaquan, Omer Tafveez, Dr. Muhammad Tahir 路 LUMS

Introducing SAE-FitNets, a student-teacher framework that injects sparse autoencoder features via decoder-weighted alignment to stabilize supervision and encourage sparsity. Gains of +2% in-distribution and +11% OOD on Style-Transfer Animals100 and TinyImagenette.

Decoupled Gradient Knowledge Distillation for OOD Generalization

Omer Tafveez, Muhammad Khaquan 路 Advisor: Dr. Muhammad Tahir

Enforces functional gradient orthogonality between target and non-target classes to improve teacher fidelity and penalize overconfidence. Achieved +6% on standard datasets and +15% on OOD benchmarks (TinyImagenette, Animals100, CIFAR-100).

Key-Driven Grouped Query Attention

Zohaib Khan, Omer Tafveez, Muhammad Khaquan, Dr. Muhammad Tahir 路 LUMS

DGQA assigns query groups via key-norm evolution with EMA smoothing to reduce noise and stabilize inter-group communication. Delivers +3% accuracy on ViT-L across CIFAR-100, TinyImageNet, and Food101, with evaluations on GPT-NeoX, GPT-2, and T5.
