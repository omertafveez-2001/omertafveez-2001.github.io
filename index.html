Research & Publications

Nov 2024 - Mar 2025

Decoupled Gradient Knowledge Distillation

Associated Lab: CITY <at> LUMS

Advisor: Dr. Muhammad Tahir -- Collaborators: Muhammad Khaquan Sheikh

  • Engineered gradient-based decoupled distillation that maximizes MSE between the gradients of target and non-target class losses,
    enhancing orthogonal learning and functional gradient independence.
  • The method acts as a regularizer, mitigating overconfidence, encouraging fidelity, and accelerating feature compaction.
  • Achieved 6% increase and up to 15% gain on OOD data over standard decoupled distillation across ViT, MobileNet, ResNet, and VGG.
Code Repository

June 2024 - Aug 2025

Key Driven Grouped Query Attention

Associated Lab: CSaLT

Advisor: Dr. Agha Ali Raza -- Collaborators: Muhammad Khaquan Sheikh, Zohaib Khan

  • Built Dynamic Grouped Query Attention with window-based norm updates, assigning queries to keys by changes in key norms.
  • Implemented EMA-based key-norm evolution, smoothing rapid changes and reducing noise, leading to a +3% accuracy gain on ViT-Large across CIFAR-100, TinyImageNet, and Food101.
  • Further evaluated on GPT-NEOx, GPT-2, and T5.
